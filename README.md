## üéØ How to Play

### Starting a Game

1. **Launch GTNW**
2. **Select Era** - Choose any year from 1789 to 2025
3. **Select President** - Pick from 47 administrations
4. **Choose Difficulty:**
   - Easy - Friendly AI, fewer crises
   - Normal - Balanced gameplay
   - Hard - Aggressive AI, frequent crises
   - Nightmare - Adaptive AI, constant pressure

### The Game Loop

**Your Turn (Simple & Streamlined):**

1. **Select Target Country**
   - Click green country picker button
   - Choose from 195 nations
   - View their stats, relations, nuclear status

2. **Choose Your Action:**
   - **Shadow President Button** (132 diplomatic/military/covert actions)
   - **Nuclear Strike Button** (choose 1-50 warheads)
   - **Declare War Button** (conventional conflict)
   - **Quick Category Buttons** (Diplomatic, Military, Covert, Economic)

3. **Action Executes Automatically**
   - Your action happens immediately
   - Turn ends automatically (no manual clicking)
   - Watch terminal for results

4. **AI Countries Take Their Turns**
   - All AI opponents act simultaneously
   - Watch event terminal for their actions
   - [T#] turn numbers track everything
   - Color-coded by severity

5. **NEW: The Living Room May Activate**
   - Enemy leader may CALL you
   - Voice conversation interface appears
   - Respond to their threats/requests
   - Affects relations and future actions

6. **NEW: Predictive Intelligence Updates**
   - Check intelligence dashboard
   - See updated war predictions
   - Monitor DEFCON forecast
   - Identify emerging threats

7. **Handle Crises (Every 3-5 Turns)**
   - Historical or AI-generated crisis appears
   - Read description and context
   - Consult advisors
   - Choose from 4 options
   - NEW: Crisis cinematics play
   - Watch consequences unfold

8. **NEW: News Coverage**
   - Check Living World News
   - See how CNN, FOX, RT covered your actions
   - Compare bias across outlets
   - AI-generated photos for each story

9. **Manage Diplomatic Messages**
   - Click Messages card (shows unread count)
   - Accept or Decline each message
   - NEW: Messages may be voice-acted
   - Relations change based on responses

10. **NEW: Check Sentiment Map**
    - See emotional states of all nations
    - "Russia is VENGEFUL"
    - "China is EMBOLDENED"
    - Plan strategy around emotions

11. **Next Turn Begins**

## üêõ Troubleshooting

### "AI opponents seem basic"

**Solution:** Install Ollama for intelligent AI
```bash
brew install ollama
ollama serve
ollama pull mistral
# In GTNW: Settings ‚Üí AI Backend ‚Üí Ollama ‚Üí mistral
```

### "Voice features not working"

**Check:**
- F5-TTS installed: `pip install f5-tts-mlx`
- Reference audio exists in voice library
- Audio permissions granted to app
- Fallback: System TTS will be used

### "Propaganda not generating"

**Check:**
- SwarmUI running on localhost:7801, OR
- OpenAI API key configured, OR
- Other image gen backend available
- Settings ‚Üí Image Generation ‚Üí Select backend

### "Predictive Intelligence seems inaccurate"

**Note:** Predictions are probabilistic
- 87% doesn't mean certain
- Real intelligence has uncertainty
- Confidence levels shown
- Accuracy improves with Ollama LLM

### "The Living Room not appearing"

**Triggers:**
- Take major action (war, sanctions, nuclear threat)
- Enemy has grievances against you
- Relations below -30
- 10-20% chance per major action
- Can force: Systems ‚Üí The Living Room ‚Üí Initiate Call

### "Adaptive AI still feels easy"

**Explanation:**
- Needs 50+ actions to build profile
- Fully adapts after 100+ actions
- Play more games
- Check Settings ‚Üí Player Profile to see adaptation level

### "Natural Language Intel not understanding"

**Tips:**
- Be specific: "Which countries are hostile to US?"
- Use country names from game
- Check suggested queries for examples
- Requires Ollama or cloud LLM backend
