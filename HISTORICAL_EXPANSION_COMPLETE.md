   - Cabinet: James Madison (State), Albert Gallatin (Treasury)

   - Cabinet: James Monroe (State)

   - Cabinet: John Quincy Adams (State)

   - Cabinet: Henry Clay (State)
   - Era: Internal improvements

7. **Andrew Jackson** (1829-1837) - Old Hickory
   - Crises: Indian Removal Act, Nullification Crisis, Bank War, Texas independence

9. **William Henry Harrison** (1841) - Died after 31 days

10. **John Tyler** (1841-1845) - Texas annexation
    - Cabinet: Daniel Webster (State)

11. **James K. Polk** (1845-1849) - Manifest Destiny
    - Crises: Oregon Territory (54-40), Mexican-American War, Veracruz landing

12. **Zachary Taylor** (1849-1850) - War hero, died in office

13. **Millard Fillmore** (1850-1853) - Compromise of 1850

14. **Franklin Pierce** (1853-1857) - Kansas-Nebraska disaster

15. **James Buchanan** (1857-1861) - Pre-Civil War paralysis

#### Civil War Era (1861-1877) - 3 Presidents
16. **Abraham Lincoln** (1861-1865) - Saved the Union
    - Cabinet: William Seward (State), Edwin Stanton (War), Ulysses Grant (General)
    - Crises: Fort Sumter, Emancipation timing, Trent Affair, Habeas corpus suspension, Gettysburg peace offer, Assassination

17. **Andrew Johnson** (1865-1869) - Reconstruction struggle
    - Cabinet: William Seward (State - continued)
    - Impeached but acquitted

18. **Ulysses S. Grant** (1869-1877) - Civil War hero
    - Cabinet: Hamilton Fish (State)
    - Reconstruction enforcer

#### Gilded Age (1877-1901) - 7 Presidents
19. **Rutherford B. Hayes** (1877-1881) - Ended Reconstruction
20. **James A. Garfield** (1881) - Assassinated after 6 months
21. **Chester A. Arthur** (1881-1885) - Civil service reform
22. **Grover Cleveland** (1885-1889) - First term, anti-imperialism
23. **Benjamin Harrison** (1889-1893) - Navy expansion
24. **Grover Cleveland** (1893-1897) - Second term (non-consecutive)
25. **William McKinley** (1897-1901) - Spanish-American War
    - Cabinet: John Hay (State), Theodore Roosevelt (Asst. Navy Secretary)
    - Assassinated 1901

#### Progressive Era (1901-1921) - 3 Presidents
26. **Theodore Roosevelt** (1901-1909) - Big Stick Diplomacy
    - Cabinet: Elihu Root (State), William Taft (War)
    - Crises: Panama Canal revolution, Great White Fleet, Coal strike, Russo-Japanese mediation

27. **William Howard Taft** (1909-1913) - Dollar Diplomacy
    - Cabinet: Philander Knox (State)

28. **Woodrow Wilson** (1913-1921) - WW1 Leader
    - Cabinet: Robert Lansing (State)
    - Crises: Lusitania, Zimmermann telegram, WW1 entry, Versailles Treaty, League of Nations, Red Scare

#### Roaring 20s & Depression (1921-1945) - 4 Presidents
29. **Warren G. Harding** (1921-1923) - Return to normalcy
    - Cabinet: Herbert Hoover (Commerce)

30. **Calvin Coolidge** (1923-1929) - Silent Cal, prosperity
    - Cabinet: Frank Kellogg (State - Nobel Prize)

31. **Herbert Hoover** (1929-1933) - Great Depression
    - Cabinet: Henry Stimson (State)
    - Crises: Stock market crash, Bonus Army, Japan invades Manchuria

32. **Franklin D. Roosevelt** (1933-1945) - Depression & WW2
    - Cabinet: Cordell Hull (State), Henry Stimson (War), George Marshall (Chief of Staff)
    - Crises: Bank Holiday, Court packing, Lend-Lease, Pearl Harbor, Japanese internment, Stalin demands, Yalta, Manhattan Project
